About Lynnwood

Lynnwood, a city nestled in the heart of the Pretoria East, embodies the essence of a vibrant and thriving community. Located an hour north of Seattle, Lynnwood boasts a rich tapestry of culture, business, and natural beauty. With the total population of 250,000, and a steady growth of 3% annually over the past five years. Lynnwood offers three major hospitals and numerous clinics provide excellent healthcare services, 15 parks, 5 hiking trails, and a beautiful waterfront for outdoor enthusiasts, low crime rate and family-friendly environment contribute to a high quality of life, active volunteer community with opportunities in education, environmental conservation, and social services.
